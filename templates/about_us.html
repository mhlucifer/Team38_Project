Our Mission

At Invatoad, we're dedicated to providing Australians with the tools and knowledge they need to combat the relentless spread of invasive cane toads. Invatoad is driven by a shared commitment to environmental stewardship and biodiversity conservation. We recognize the urgent need to confront the invasion of cane toads and their profound impact on Australia's delicate ecosystems.

Our platform is your go-to resource for understanding, managing, and addressing the challenges posed by these invasive species. Through education, awareness, and innovative features, we aim to empower individuals and communities to take meaningful action against this invasive species.
