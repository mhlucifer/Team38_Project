InvaToad: Unveiling the Impact of Australia's Cane Toad Infestation

Empowering Change, Safeguarding Australia's Wildlife

Did you know?

  • Cane toads have become a significant threat to Australian ecosystems.
  • Did you know? The rate of cane toad sightings is increasing by approximately 15% to 20% every year!
  • Australia is losing biodiversity at an alarming rate of about 1% to 1.5% annually, with invasive species like cane toads contributing significantly.
  • Over 250 native species in Australia have been adversely affected by the presence of cane toads, highlighting the critical need for conservation efforts.
